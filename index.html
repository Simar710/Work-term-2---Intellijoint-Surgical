My Employer

My Employer was Intellijoint Surgical

Intellijoint Surgical is a Canadian medical device company that provides minimally invasive, patient-specific joint replacement surgery. It manufactures and markets surgical navigation systems that enable orthopedic surgeons to provide precision in joint replacement surgery. The company's technology uses a combination of 3D imaging and advanced analytics to create a 3D model of the patient's anatomy, allowing the surgeon to plan, navigate, and perform the surgery with greater accuracy. The company's technology is designed to reduce the risk of complications associated with joint replacement surgery, and to improve patient outcomes.
